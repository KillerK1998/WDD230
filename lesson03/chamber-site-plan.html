Saint George Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Serving as a beacon for business growth and community development in Saint George.

Site Purpose

The Saint George Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to:

  • Enhancing business visibility and networking opportunities.
  • Advocating for public policies that support economic growth.

Common Questions

  • What networking events are available this month?
  • How can I contact the Chamber's board of directors?

Typography

The selected font for the website is Arial sans-serif, which provides a clean and modern look. Different font weights are used to emphasize various elements:

  • Regular (400) for body text.
  • Bold (700) for headings and important information.
